Brief Description

3D Artwork is easier than it looks. Grab a canvas and pour different colors of paint on the canvas for the background. Mix the paint around to get the style of your choice. Then search up some origami projects online and complete them. Then tape the origami to the canvas. Ta-da

Time Length

40-50mins

Materials

  • Canvas
  • Paint
  • Origami Paper

Brief Description

Baking cookies is a great activity to do with friends, family or by yourself. Mix 1 egg, 1/2 cups sugar, 1/2 cups flour, and 1 cup peanut butter together and bake it in the oven for 15mins. Makes 24 small cookies.

Time Length

30-40mins

Materials

  • Peanut Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour

Brief Description

Are you wanting to grow something but are bored of the typical plant? If so then try an aquatic plant. Marimo balls are easy to take care of and can be petted. All you need to do is change the water every two weeks. Marimo balls can be housed in any clear jar.

Time Length

10mins

Materials

  • Marimo Ball
  • Clear Jar
  • Water

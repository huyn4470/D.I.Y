Brief Description

Cactus are great for decorations. They brighten up the room and are a very hardy plant, requiring minimal care. To plant a cactus, grab a pot, some soil, and a cactus seed. Water bi-weekly. Cactus seeds can be purchased online or in garden centers.

Time Length

20-25mins

Materials

  • Pot
  • Soil
  • Cactus
  • Water
